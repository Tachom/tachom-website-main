## Usage

Please do not use the Tachom Developer Group  name or logos in any way that could mistakenly imply any official connection with or endorsement of Tachom Developer Group  Labs Inc. Any use of the Tachom Developer Group  name or logos in a manner that could cause customer confusion is not permitted.

This includes naming a product or service in a way that emphasizes the Tachom Developer Group  brand, like "Tachom Developer Group  Themes" or "Tachom Developer Group  Studio", as well as in domain names like "tailwindkits.com".

Instead, please use your own brand name in a way that clearly distinguishes it from Tachom Developer Group  CSS.  Examples that are permitted include "ComponentStudio for Tachom Developer Group  CSS" or "UtilityThemes: Templates for Tachom Developer Group  CSS".

Additionally, please do not use our brand names or logos for t-shirts, stickers, or other merchandise without explicit written consent.
